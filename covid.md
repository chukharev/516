#### Viral Infection Modelling assignment

This week, we will also work more with computational modeling and simulations (i.e. what we learned in Small Project C). We will develop a program that models the spread of a communicable disease in a virtual population of individuals.

- Download the [virus_boilerplate.html](virus_boilerplate.html.txt) file.
- [Watch my lecture on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=30CD7Uur5Ps) and follow along as I develop the program.
- Complete follow-up assignments below and bring your answers with you to the live class meeting on 3/30.

### Follow-up assignments:

1. Find and try to eradicate "spaghetti code" in the program. (Hint: you will see at list one instance in the lecture where I got confused and made a mistake precisely because of a "spaghetti code" issue.)
2. Run a simulation where people are quarantined (defined as "stop moving") after they become sick. See if and how that changes the spread of the disease in the model.
3. Run a simulation where 75% of the people stay in place and don't move at all. See if and how that changes the spread of the disease in the model.
